A small game like that:
	# 2 players will play with 3 stone piles. Each stone pile has n<=100 stones
	# In player A's turn, A can get k>0 stones in one of 3 stone piles.
	# 2 players will get stones until 3 stone piles is clear
	# Finally, winner is who play last turn

This program used reinforcement learning to train a AI player to play this game.

Environment: Python 3.7.1, numpy, pickle.
